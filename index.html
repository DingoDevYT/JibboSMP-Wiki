Welcome to the Unofficial Server Wiki

This wiki is dedicated to documenting the builds, history, and key locations of our Beta 1.7.3 Minecraft server. Whether you are looking for the nearest marketplace, exploring ancient ruins, or just trying to find your way back to Spawn, we've got you covered.

Note: We are not the server administrators! This is a community-run project to help map out the world.
